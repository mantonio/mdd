Attending Full Sail University has to be one of the best decisions I have made recently. When you learn a bit more about me, you'll come to find that my previous endeavors just weren't for me. The only thing I regret is not knowing what I really wanted to do earlier.

The classes at Full Sail have taught me more than I expected and given me the opportunity to show creativity I didn't even know I had. A lot of the work below consists of graphic design which happens to play a very vital role in web design. Yes, some of the projects were also done by other students, but they're all unique to the look and feel I created.
